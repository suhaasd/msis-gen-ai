### How to Get Cited by Google AI Overviews: A Marketer’s Guide (2025)
- https://www.aeochecker.ai/blogs/how-to-get-cited-by-google-ai-overviews

AI Overviews (AIO) have reached ~13% of searches in March 2025, skewing toward informational intent.12 When an AI summary appears, users click less overall, and citations skew toward Wikipedia, YouTube, and Reddit. This means your content must be quotable and credible inside the answer, not just ranked below it.3

Google’s guidance is clear: there is no special markup to force inclusion in AI Overviews. Focus on helpful, reliable, people-first content, solid technical hygiene, and structured data to improve understanding (not a guarantee of display).45
